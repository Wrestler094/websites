Vertical Navigation Bars

Here are some important items to remember:

  • The body holds a nav tag and then a section tag that holds everything else.
  • The height is 100vh = 100% of viewport
    • vh and vw are the new units in CSS3. See this: Sizing with CSS3's vw and vh units
    • vh = Viewport Height
    • vw = Viewport Width
