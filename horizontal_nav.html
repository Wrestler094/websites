Horizontal Navigation Bars

Here are some important items to remember:

  • Just like the vertical nav page, the body holds a nav tag and then a section tag that holds everything else.
  • The height is 100vh = 100% of viewport
    • vh = Viewport Height
    • vw = Viewport Width
  • The "Home" link also has the "current" class to indicate to the user that they are on that page
  • The hover pseudo-class is also used for just the links in the nav.
